# Relationship Pair Sheets

## Bond: Betty ↔ Tim
- Bond Type: Emotional/social office alliance
- Mutual or One-Way: Mutual, but Betty leads socially
- Surface Read: Work wife / work husband energy
- Hidden Truth: They lean on each other for emotional relief and attention, but the bond is not as principled as it looks
- Bond Strength: 90
- Sentiment Transfer Strength: 80
- Main Weakness: Jealousy, humiliation, selfishness under pressure
- Main Reinforcement: Shared gossip, mutual validation, private venting
- Most Likely Break Trigger: One of them protecting themselves at the other’s expense
- Most Likely Loyalty Trigger: Feeling attacked from outside
- Notes:
  - Strongest social pair in the office
  - Good target for split-pressure strategies
