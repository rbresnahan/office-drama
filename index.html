Quick Terms

People

Read on People
Your current visible read on each coworker. Not the full truth. Just the practical state you can act on.
Target
The coworker the email is about.
Open
More receptive and easier to influence.
Settled
Stable and not especially alarmed.
Guarded
Cautious. Not relaxed, not fully against you.
Watchful
Actively suspicious and looking for motive.
Hostile
Against you and willing to make things worse for you.

Target awareness

Unaware
The target does not know.
At risk
The target is close to finding out.
Suspicious
The target senses something is wrong.
Confirmed
The target knows. Clean prevention is over.

Run state

Containment
You are still trying to stop or limit discovery.
Defensive
The target knows or the run has shifted into fallout control.
All-hands
The public meeting phase. Free-roam manipulation is over.
Background
The issue exists, but is not running the room yet.
Active
The issue is clearly shaping behavior.
Dominant
The room is now mainly about this problem.

Delay discovery. Shape the room. Survive the reveal.

Reply All

You sent a bad email about one coworker. Recall only partly worked. Keep them from finding out too early—and if they do, survive the fallout.
